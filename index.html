About the Program

AlliedCyber Mentor is a hands-on cybersecurity mentorship program built to guide students, professionals, and certification candidates through the skills, strategies, and support needed to succeed in real-world cyber careers. Our focus is mentorship—not just lectures.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✔️ One-on-one and small group mentorship sessions
  • ✔️ Certification preparation (CISSP, CC, CCSP, CCSE and more)
  • ✔️ Resume building, LinkedIn polishing, and career coaching
  • ✔️ Weekly live instruction + project-based learning
  • ✔️ Community support and job referral connections
  • ✔️ Affordable pricing for students, veterans, and career switchers
